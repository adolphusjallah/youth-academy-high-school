About Us

A School Built on Purpose

Youth Academy High School was established in 2011 with a clear mission: to provide every young Liberian student with a quality education that equips them for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century. From humble beginnings, YAHS has grown into a thriving academic community of over 367 students across 12 grade levels.

2011 — Youth Academy High School founded in Ganta City, Nimba County, Liberia.
2013 — First graduating class of 28 students. 100% passed West African Examinations.
2016 — Expanded to Grade 1–12 full school offering. Science laboratory opened.
2020 — Launched digital learning program; maintained continuity through pandemic period.
2026 — Enrollment exceeds 367 students. ERP management system launched.
🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality secondary education that develops the intellectual, moral, and social potential of every student, preparing them for higher education and responsible citizenship.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the leading secondary school in Liberia recognized for academic excellence, strong values, and graduates who lead transformative change in their communities and nation.

⭐

Our Promise

Every YAHS student will graduate with the knowledge, skills, and character needed to contribute meaningfully to their family, profession, and the Republic of Liberia.

What We Stand For

Values & Philosophy

Our values are not words on a wall. They are the living principles that guide every teacher's lesson, every student's decision, and every interaction within the YAHS community.

"Discipline. Excellence. Service. Leadership."
— The YAHS Motto
📖

Academic Integrity

We hold every student to the highest standard of honesty in their work. Intellectual honesty is the foundation of true learning and lifelong credibility.

🤝

Respect & Dignity

Every person on our campus — student, teacher, staff, or visitor — is treated with respect and dignity. We celebrate our diversity as a strength.

🏅

Excellence

We reject mediocrity. Students are challenged to go beyond their comfort zone and are supported to produce their personal best in everything they do.

🌱

Growth Mindset

We believe every student can improve. Mistakes are learning opportunities. Teachers model curiosity and resilience alongside the students they teach.

🫂

Community Service

YAHS graduates are expected to give back. Community service is embedded in our program — students learn that leadership means serving others first.

⚖️

Accountability

Students, teachers, and leadership hold themselves accountable to their commitments and responsibilities — this is the core of personal character development.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At Youth Academy, we put that weapon in the hands of every student who walks through our doors."
— YAHS School Principal
"Our educational philosophy is simple: teach students how to think, not what to think. A student who can reason, communicate, and collaborate is prepared for any future."
— YAHS Academic Dean
